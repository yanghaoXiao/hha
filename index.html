The 3rd International Conference on AI and Data Science (AIDSW 2025)

Beijing, China, June 15-18, 2025

The 3rd International Conference on AI and Data Science (AIDSW 2025)

This accepted conference aims to explore how artificial intelligence and data science can drive innovation and development across various industries. Accepted papers will be presented at the conference and included in the IEEE ICML 2025 conference proceedings.

Call for Papers

With the rapid development of technology, artificial intelligence and data science have become key forces driving industry innovation. Breakthroughs in deep learning, reinforcement learning, natural language processing, and computer vision have provided new possibilities for solving complex problems. Meanwhile, the availability of large-scale data has enabled researchers to develop more intelligent and efficient algorithms to address various practical challenges.

This conference aims to provide a platform for researchers to share the latest research findings in artificial intelligence and data science and their innovative applications across various fields. The conference will focus on challenges and opportunities in algorithmic innovation, model optimization, ethical considerations, and practical deployment. In addition, we welcome papers describing open-source datasets and tools that will help promote community solutions to fundamental technical problems in the fields of artificial intelligence and data science.

Topics

Conference topics include but are not limited to:

  • Deep Learning Algorithms and Applications
  • Large-scale Machine Learning Systems
  • Natural Language Processing and Understanding
  • Computer Vision and Image Analysis
  • Reinforcement Learning and Decision Systems
  • Graph Neural Networks and Knowledge Graphs
  • Time Series Analysis and Prediction
  • Multimodal Learning Methods
  • Self-supervised and Semi-supervised Learning
  • Generative Artificial Intelligence
  • Federated Learning and Privacy Protection
  • Explainable AI
  • AI Ethics and Fairness
  • Edge Computing and AI Hardware Acceleration
  • AI Applications in Scientific Research
  • Data Science in Business Decision-making
